Sanford

sanford is a hyperactive and cheerful skeleton, they lives in a daycare and helps the caregivers with the little ones who visit them. they likes to play catch, hide and seek and hot potato. Their favorite food is glitter glue and that makes their body shine.

Valentino

When Valentino was born, he was the promotional image of the Daycare, but after a while he stopped being excited to see the children and do shows, after an escape attempt, he met Sanford, a skeleton identical to the one that slept in the file room of the Daycare. Over time, they two became friends, Valentino made his presentations with ideas that Sanford gave him, until one day Valentino disappeared and Sanford took his place as the Daycare's mascot, nobody knows where he went but nobody seems to remember him.
